Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

"AliExpress Orders Report" ("the extension") is a browser extension that helps you export your AliExpress order history to Excel (XLSX) or CSV files.

Data Collection

The extension does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data, browsing history, or order information with us or any third party.

How the Extension Works

When you click "Export Orders," the extension reads order details (date, order number, status, product names, price, and quantity) directly from the AliExpress Orders page that is currently open in your browser. This data is processed entirely on your device and is used only to generate the Excel or CSV file that is downloaded to your computer.

Data Storage

No data is stored by the extension beyond the local file you choose to save. The extension does not use cookies, analytics, or remote servers.

Permissions

The extension requests the minimum permissions required to read the active AliExpress tab and save the exported file (activeTab, scripting, downloads, and access to aliexpress.com).

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer any data to third parties, because no data is collected in the first place.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.

Contact
